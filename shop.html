How It Works

From Cart To Closet

Real talk: we're just a bunch of normal people who love this game and love life. This isn't a money grab — nobody here is buying a boat off glizzy hoodies. Whatever we make goes right back into growing something real.

The goal is simple: bring the community back together like it's the '90s again — back when you knew your neighbors, the rink smelled like victory, and a hot dog cost a buck. Some of us are unc now. Some of us were teenie weenies back then. Doesn't matter. Every hat, tee, and jersey you grab helps us build it.

Thanks for rocking with us. Let's do this together. 🌭

Questions

Shop FAQ

How does shipping work?

Jerseys, hats, pucks, and stickers ship free. Everything else (hoodies and tees) has a low per-item rate calculated at checkout based on your order and address. International orders are calculated at checkout too.

Is checkout secure?

Yes. Checkout is handled by Shopify with encrypted, PCI-compliant payment processing. We never see your card details.

How long until it ships?

Tees, hoodies, stickers, pucks, beanies, and the links wiper are printed on demand and ship straight to your door — no long wait. Jerseys and hats are limited-time presale items: you reserve and pay now, then we place the batch order. Jerseys ship roughly 4–6 weeks after the presale closes; hats are en route from our supplier and ship in about 8–10 weeks. Each presale item lists its own timing on the product page.

Can I customize my jersey?

Yes — the Custom Jersey lets you add your nameplate and number, embroidered on the back. Double-check your spelling before you add to cart.

What's the return policy?

All sales final. We've only got so many leashes for so many dogs — we're here to get Glizzies out into the wild, not run a returns desk. Order your size carefully and give that dog a good home. (If something shows up damaged or flat-out wrong, email us and we'll make it right.)

What's Founder's Pricing?

The current jersey price is locked in for founding Glizzies ordering now. Pricing goes up once the Foundation has been built.
