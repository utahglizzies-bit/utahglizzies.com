Questions

Presale FAQ

Is this a real purchase?

No payment is collected today. This is a pledge to show demand. We'll contact you when it's time to pay — you decide then if you still want in.

Why does price depend on pledges?

Higher volume = lower per-unit cost from the manufacturer. More Glizzies fans pledging means everyone pays less. Spread the word.

How long does delivery take?

Approximately 8 weeks from when production is confirmed and payment collected. Full embroidery takes time — this is not a mass-produced drop.

Can I back out?

Yes. Pledges are non-binding. If we reach out and you've changed your mind, no problem. No charge, no hard feelings — just fewer Glizzies in the wild.

What if you don't hit enough pledges?

If we can't reach minimum production volume, we'll let everyone know and no payment will ever be collected. You're on the list for the next attempt.

Can I pledge for multiple items?

Absolutely. Select "Both" or add notes in the form about quantities. We'll confirm everything with you before payment.
