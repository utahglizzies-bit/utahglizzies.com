Presale FAQ

Is this a real purchase?

No payment collected today. This is a pledge to show demand. Hats are $35, blank jerseys (logo only) are $70, and custom jerseys with name + number are $105. We'll contact you to collect payment when pledges close.

What is the return policy?

All sales are final. Once your merchandise has been received, we do not accept returns or exchanges. Custom jerseys are made to order with your name and number — make sure everything looks right before submitting your pledge.

How much does everything cost?

Hats are $35, blank jerseys are $70, and custom jerseys are $105 — plus taxes at checkout. Pledging confirms the production run — you only pay when your gear ships.

How long does delivery take?

Approximately 8 weeks from when production is confirmed and payment collected. Full embroidery takes time — this is not a mass-produced drop.

Can I back out?

Yes. Pledges are non-binding. If we reach out and you've changed your mind, no problem. No charge, no hard feelings — just fewer Glizzies in the wild.

What if you don't hit enough pledges?

If we can't reach minimum production volume, we'll let everyone know and no payment will ever be collected. You're on the list for the next attempt.

Can I pledge for multiple items?

Absolutely. Select "Both" or add notes in the form about quantities. We'll confirm everything with you before payment.
