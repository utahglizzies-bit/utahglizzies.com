Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the strongest hot dog-based hockey brand in Utah.

Utah Glizzies HC is a beer league ice hockey team based in Sandy, Utah, playing at Utah Mammoth Ice Center. We combine competitive hockey with the strongest hot dog-based brand in Utah — complete with player lore, playoff drama, and an embarrassing amount of mustard energy.

All home games are played at Utah Mammoth Ice Center, located at 10450 S. State Street STE 2200A, Sandy, UT 84070. Most games are on North or South Rink. Check the schedule page for exact rink assignments per game.

Beer league hockey is adult recreational ice hockey. No checking, competitive but fun, and open to players of varying skill levels. The Utah Glizzies compete in the D League division at Mammoth Ice Center.

Games are open for friends, family, and fans to attend at Utah Mammoth Ice Center. Follow @utah_glizzies_hockey on Instagram for game-night updates, photos, and reels. The full schedule with dates, times, and rink info is on this site.

We offer four sponsor tiers from Mustard Packet to Glizzy Legend. Sponsors get site presence, social shoutouts, and the undying loyalty of a beer league team. Email utahglizzies@gmail.com with the subject 'Sponsorship Opportunity' or visit the Sponsors page.

The Glizzies occasionally add affiliate subs and may expand the roster. If you are a skater looking to join the best hot dog-themed hockey brand in Utah, email utahglizzies@gmail.com. Nameplates are assigned by the council after a thorough vibe assessment.
