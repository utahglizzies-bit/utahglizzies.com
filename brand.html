04 — Usage Guidelines

Dos & Don'ts

✅ Do

  • Use provided PNG files for all placements
  • Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "G" in Glizzies
  • Use only the approved color palette in co-branded materials
  • Contact us before applying the logo to physical merchandise or signage
  • Reproduce the wordmark at legible sizes (min 80px wide digital, min 1" print)

❌ Don't

  • Stretch, skew, rotate, or distort any logo or wordmark
  • Change logo colors or apply color overlays
  • Place the logo on a background without sufficient contrast
  • Use off-brand fonts in sponsor materials referencing the team
  • Recreate the logo or wordmark from screenshots or memory
