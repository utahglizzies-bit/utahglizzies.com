Shop FAQ

How does shipping work?

Free shipping on any order over $120 — mix and match anything in the shop to get there. Under $120, shipping is calculated at checkout based on your order and address.

Is checkout secure?

Yes. Checkout is handled by Shopify with encrypted, PCI-compliant payment processing. We never see your card details.

How long until it ships?

Tees, hoodies, stickers, pucks, and beanies are made-to-order and typically ship within a couple of weeks. Jerseys are fully embroidered and made to order — allow extra time. Hats ship from on-hand stock.

Can I customize my jersey?

Yes — the Custom Jersey lets you add your nameplate and number, embroidered on the back. Double-check your spelling before you add to cart.

What's the return policy?

Made-to-order and custom items are final sale — we build each one for you specifically. If something arrives damaged or wrong, email us and we'll make it right.

What's Founder's Pricing?

The current jersey price is locked in for founding Glizzies ordering now. Pricing goes up once the Foundation has been built.
