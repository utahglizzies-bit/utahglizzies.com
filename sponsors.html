Usage Guidelines

✅ Do

  • Use the wordmark on approved sponsor materials
  • Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the cap height
  • Use Glizzy Green or Rink Ink as background colors
  • Contact us before applying the logo to merchandise

❌ Don’t

  • Stretch, rotate, or distort the logo
  • Place the logo on busy backgrounds without contrast
  • Use unofficial colors or fonts in co-branded materials
  • Recreate the logo from scratch — use provided files
