The Origin Story

Zimmy Casino was discovered in the back room of a suspicious rink-side casino where the only games were air hockey, pull-tabs, and betting on whether the vending machine would actually drop your snack. Zimmy never lost. Not once. He could read bounces off the boards like marked cards. Now Gliz-Z plays like every shift is a high-stakes hand, bluffing defenders and cashing in rebounds.
